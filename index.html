Do You Haiku?

Do you want to know if your poem is a haiku? Well, now you can! Just enter your poem in the form, and we'll let you know if you have a haiku.

Note on form:

  • The philosophy of the haiku is simplicity and brevity and therefore, traditionally haikus do not have titles.
  • Haikus consist of 17 syllables and 3 lines in the following format:
    • The first line has 5 syllables
    • The second line has 7 syllables
    • The third line has 5 syllables
